❓ How to Play

🧙 You start top-left. Reach the 🚪 exit at bottom-right.

🗝️ Collect 3 keys to unlock the exit.

💰 Grab coins for bonus score (value = level × 10 pts).

💀 Guards patrol the dungeon — touching one costs 1 HP.

⚠️ Hidden traps look like floor tiles — step to reveal (-1 HP).

❤️ You have 3 HP. Reach 0 = game over.

🏰 10 levels total — guards get faster each level.

🏆 Bonus score: coins + time saved + remaining HP!
